History of Reader's World

I began this page as a step to spread awareness of reading. I have heard countless times that people don't like to read, and it made me upset since reading is so wonderful and I feel bad that they are left out from experiencing something so great. I then realized that a lot of people don't know where to start reading: where to get a book, what are the different genres, etc. Thus, I created this page to help people answer all those beginning questions. I started in 2022 and hope to grow this page in the year to come.
